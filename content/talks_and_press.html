--- Press & Talks : --- ### Paper #### Zerocoin: Anonymous Distributed E-Cash from Bitcoin #####Ian Miers, Christina Garman, Matthew Green, Aviel D. Rubin [IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy (Oakland) 2013](http://www.ieee-security.org/TC/SP2013/) | [PDF]({{media_url('pdf/ZerocoinOakland.pdf')}})| Abstract: > Bitcoin is the ﬁrst e-cash system to see widespread > adoption. While Bitcoin offers the potential for new types of > ﬁnancial interaction, it has signiﬁcant limitations regarding > privacy. Speciﬁcally, because the Bitcoin transaction log is > completely public, users’ privacy is protected only through > the use of pseudonyms. In this paper we propose Zerocoin, a > cryptographic extension to Bitcoin that augments the protocol > to allow for fully anonymous currency transactions. Our system > uses standard cryptographic assumptions and does not introduce > new trusted parties or otherwise change the security model of > Bitcoin. We detail Zerocoin’s cryptographic construction, its > integration into Bitcoin, and examine its performance both in > terms of computation and impact on the Bitcoin protocol. ### Press * ['Zerocoin' Add-on For Bitcoin Could Make It Truly Anonymous And Untraceable](http://www.forbes.com/sites/andygreenberg/2013/04/12/zerocoin-add-on-for-bitcoin-could-make-it-truly-anonymous-and-untraceable/ "Forbes:Zerocoin' Add-on For Bitcoin Could Make It Truly Anonymous And Untraceable") Forbes 4.12.2013 * [Bitcoin add-on makes your virtual purchases private](http://www.newscientist.com/blogs/onepercent/2013/03/bitcoin-zerocoin.html) Newscientist 3.13.2013 ### Talks #### Ian Miers @ [IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy]("http://www.ieee-security.org/TC/SP2013/") 5.21.2013 Sadly no video as far as we know.
